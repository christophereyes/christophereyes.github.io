# Visitor Communication Strategy for Website Redesign

## Overview
The goal of the visitor communication strategy is to keep our users informed and engaged throughout the website redesign process. This will include regular updates, feedback opportunities, and support channels.

## Strategy Components
1. **Regular Updates**
   - Bi-weekly blog posts on the website to share progress and upcoming features.
   - Email newsletters to subscribers informing them of changes and inviting feedback.

2. **Feedback Opportunities**
   - Surveys sent to users to gather opinions on design concepts and features.
   - Feedback forms accessible on the website during the redesign phase.

3. **Support Channels**
   - A dedicated support email for inquiries related to the redesign.
   - Live chat feature available during business hours for immediate support.
   
## Timeline
- **Week 1-2**: Announce the redesign project and share the vision.
- **Week 3-6**: Share design concepts and gather user feedback.
- **Week 7-8**: Announce final designs and launch plans.

## Conclusion
By implementing this communication strategy, we aim to build trust with our users and ensure the redesign meets their needs and expectations.
